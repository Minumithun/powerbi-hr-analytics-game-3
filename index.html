üé® Format your visuals with consistent colors:

  5. Repeat for other visuals
3

Add Titles and Format Text

üìù Make your dashboard professional with titles:

  1. Select a visual
  2. In Format pane, find Title
  3. Turn Title ON
  4. Change title text (e.g., "Employees by Department")
  5. Repeat for all visuals
4

Add Background and Final Polish

‚ú® Final touches for a professional look:

  1. Click on empty space in your report
  2. Go to Format pane
  3. Find Page background
  4. Choose a subtle color (light gray or white)
  5. Ensure all text is readable
